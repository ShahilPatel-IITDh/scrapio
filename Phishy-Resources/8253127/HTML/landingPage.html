CMP foods is a global beef and lamb producer with established markets in Oceania, Asia, North America, the Middle East, and Europe.

The majority of CMP foods finest beef is grass-fed, due to our country’s extensive pastures and favourable climate – factors that set us apart on the world stage.

Generations of New Zealand farmers have perfected the raising of quality, healthy, lean and nutritious lamb.

CMP Foods supports farmers’ health and meets strict requirements for sustainable beef and lamb while prioritizing animal welfare and the environment.

Supplying some of the world’s largest food companies, our quality, skill and expertise means we’re trusted by customers the world over.
