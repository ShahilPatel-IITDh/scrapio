Approval Hacks & Exploits

Over $261M stolen since 2020.

Revoke Now

This page contains an list of known hacks and exploits in which approved user funds are at risk. Many other hacks and exploits only affect the funds kept in the exploited smart contract. But the exploits listed on this page abuse unlimited token approvals to steal approved funds. By using Revoke.cash to manage your allowances, you can keep your funds safe from these exploits. Revoke Now if your wallet is affected by clicking on the exploits below.
