About Ryan

Councilperson Ryan Kennedy is a parent, a dedicated volunteer, a working professional attorney and a long time civil servant working for our Borough. With the retirement of Mayor Anzano after 16 years in office, rather than seek re-election to a 3rd term on Council, Ryan Kennedy is stepping up to lead our town.

Ryan grew up in Hopewell Valley, graduated from our school system and is raising his daughters here, focusing on safety, affordability, communications and our environment.
