Welcome to the Rolling Thunder Racing Series. Are you passionate about racing but don’t have access to real tracks or race cars? Look no further! iRacing is an immersive online racing platform that brings the excitement of motorsport directly to your fingertips. It offers a realistic racing experience through cutting-edge simulation technology, allowing you to compete against other drivers from around the world.

Reasons to join Rolling Thunder Racing Series!

  1. Diverse Racing Disciplines: Rolling Thunder Racing Series offers high-speed oval racing, and a challenging road course. Get ready to showcase your skills in various racing environments and adapt to different racing styles.
  2. Competitive Racing Environment: If you crave intense competition and want to test your racing abilities against skilled drivers, Rolling Thunder Racing Series is the place to be. The league attracts passionate and dedicated racers who are committed to honing their skills and pushing the limits of their performance. You’ll have the opportunity to race against some of the best drivers in the online racing community.
  3. Well-Structured and Organized Events: Rolling Thunder Racing Series prides itself on its well-organized events. Races are meticulously planned and executed, ensuring fairness, excitement, and a smooth racing experience for all participants. From pre-race practice sessions to qualifying rounds and feature races, every aspect is thoughtfully managed to provide a professional and engaging racing atmosphere.
  4. Supportive and Friendly Community: Joining Rolling Thunder Racing Series means becoming part of a vibrant and welcoming community of racing enthusiasts. Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for motorsport and are eager to share their knowledge and experiences. The community offers a supportive environment where you can learn, improve, and build lasting friendships with fellow racers.
  5. Live Streaming and Broadcasts: Rolling Thunder Racing Series broadcasts its events through live streams, allowing you to showcase your racing skills to a broader audience. The thrill of competing in front of an audience adds an extra layer of excitement and motivation. Share your triumphs, close battles, and memorable moments with friends, family, and fellow racing fans.

So, if you’re looking for thrilling competition, a supportive community, and the chance to showcase your racing prowess, don’t miss out on the exciting opportunities that the Rolling Thunder Racing Series has to offer. Join today and start your journey towards becoming a champion on the virtual track!
