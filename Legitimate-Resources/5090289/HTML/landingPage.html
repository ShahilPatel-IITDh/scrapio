    Hotel details
    In a sleek high-rise building facing the West India Docks waterfront, this modern apartment hotel is 9 minutes' walk from Canary Wharf tube station and 3 miles from concerts and sports at MORE
    the O2 Arena.

    Featuring floor-to-ceiling windows and contemporary artwork, the warmly decorated studios provide Wi-Fi access (fee) and flat-screen TVs, plus kitchens and living areas. The 1- and 2-bedroom apartments add separate living rooms. Room service is available 24/7.

    Amenities consist of a 24-hour gym, a sauna and a bar, as well as a steakhouse with water views. Event space includes 12,023 sq ft of meeting rooms and a ballroom.
