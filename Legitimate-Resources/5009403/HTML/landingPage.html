Professional cooking tips, useful at home

You don't have to be a professional chef to be careful in every detail. Following some home, cooking tips is a great way to get more delicious results.

Many of these tips are essential for anyone who wants to dedicate himself to the gastronomic sector. However, they can be implemented at home by anyone who calls himself an enthusiast and a lover of utensils, recipes, and ingredients .

You have to pamper the knives

Having a varied and sharp set of knives is one of the most recurring kitchen tips

One of the most useful and important tools for any cook. Having well-sharpened knives is perfect for getting good cuts of meat, vegetables, and other produce. However, the edge tends to lose with use and time.

That is why one of the recommendations is to keep them very sharp, this can be done with a special implement for it or even with something simple like a stone.

always try everything

A fundamental premise, since every cook must know the state of what will be served to others. As the dish is prepared, you have to try the recipe. This will allow you to make any necessary flavor adjustments to achieve what you want. Normally salt is the input used to rectify during the process.

Likewise, it is not recommended to reintroduce the spoon or other utensils with which it was tested again in the preparation. The idea is to use another one to extract a little and then pass it to where it will be tested.

Taking care of the mise en place

Arranging both the ingredients and the utensils will facilitate the whole process

Of the most important kitchen tips. The mise en place refers to having all the utensils and products properly arranged to cook more comfortably. It's worth chopping, weighing, and dividing all the ingredients properly before you start the recipe.

The importance of cleanliness

Nobody wants to eat in a restaurant that looks dirty and unhealthy, because this also applies at home. Making sure everything is clean and tidy is something relevant for any chef.

A trick for pasta

The pasta can be cooked together with the ingredients to make everything in one go

Something that can be done to use less water is to reuse the pasta water to prepare the sauce. Since it already contains a good amount of salt, it can be a great base for another preparation.

The pasta can even be prepared together with the sauce, following the one-pot pasta trend.

Give the meat a break

Once it is cooked, it is advised to let it rest for a few minutes. Cutting it immediately will cause it to lose some of its natural juices obtained after cooking.

Cleaning the vacuum cleaner: The best tips and tricks

It doesn't matter whether it's dust bunnies, animal hair, or dirt - such a vacuum cleaner has to swallow a lot. There is no question that it deserves a thorough cleaning now and then. If this is not done, the pipe and filter can get so dirty over time that they can no longer work properly . It can also quickly happen that a vacuum cleaner starts to stink. But how do you properly clean a vacuum cleaner? We have a few tips for you.

Cleaning the vacuum cleaner: The best tips and tricks

Clean the vacuum cleaner tube

Have you noticed a loss of suction power in your vacuum cleaner? The cause of this is usually in the vacuum cleaner pipe - in the truest sense of the word. A lot of dust and dirt has often settled here over time. But don't panic, this is easy to fix: simply soak the pipe and hose in the bathtub and then rinse the parts with running water . Very important: let it dry completely!

Clean filter

Did you know that vacuum cleaner filters should generally be replaced every six months? Until then, it is important to clean it regularly. It is usually enough to simply rinse the filter with clear water . If you have a vacuum cleaner without a bag, the filter should of course also be cleaned regularly - with bagless vacuum cleaners, they get dirty even more often!

Clean floor nozzle

Here we are dealing with a real boss: The brushes of the floor nozzle are often very dirty. No wonder hair, lint, and dust tend to get caught here. It is best to regularly pluck off this dirt to avoid sticking or crusting . You can also use a comb here or vacuum the brush with the vacuum cleaner tube after each vacuum cleaning process. In the case of particularly heavy soiling, it is also advisable to remove the vacuum cleaner brush and soak it in warm water. You are also welcome to add a mild soap-based detergent .

Tip: If you like, you can also use household remedies when cleaning the vacuum cleaner brush . Shaving foam, for example, has proven its worth. Let this take effect on the vacuum cleaner brush and then rinse everything off with clear water.

Clean the case

Of course, the housing of the vacuum cleaner must also be cleaned now and then. It is often sufficient to briefly wipe the plastic parts of the vacuum cleaner with a damp cloth and a small splash of washing-up liquid . Regions that only come to light when the bag or filter is changed should of course not be ignored: For example, the collection container of bagless models must be emptied and wiped regularly. For stubborn dirt, you can wash it under running water.

Clean vacuum cleaner hose

Yes, the vacuum cleaner hose can also become clogged. To free it from deposits of all kinds, it is often enough to simply tap it out . In case of doubt, however, the same applies here: Off to the bathtub with it! Let the water run through until no more dirt runs out. Then dry well.

How often should you clean the vacuum cleaner?

After cleaning is before cleaning! In general, you should disassemble your vacuum cleaner into its parts and clean it thoroughly once a year. You can also find specific maintenance information in the operating instructions.
