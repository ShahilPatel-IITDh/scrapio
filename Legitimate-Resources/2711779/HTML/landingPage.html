CHECK-UPS AND CLEANINGS

Our professional hygienists gently clean and assess your teeth and gums, removing tartar and plaque, and polishing your teeth, leaving you with a cleaner and healthier smile. Cleaning is a key part of an effective dental health routine.

ORAL CANCER SCREENING

Maxwell Heights Dental performs oral cancer screening to look for signs of cancer or precancerous conditions in your mouth. Oral cancer screening is an important tool in preventative health and early cancer detection.

EARLY ORTHODONTIC SCREENING

Regular maintenance and check-ups allow us to monitor the growth and development of patients teeth. We identify personal dental needs from an early stage and help patients plan effectively when orthodontics are required.

PERIODONTAL TREATMENT

Periodontal disease is an infection of the gums, often caused by plaque buildup on teeth. Periodontal therapy includes both surgical and non-surgical techniques to restore health to the tissues that support your teeth.

ROOT CANAL TREATMENT
