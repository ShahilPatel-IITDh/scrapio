Are you looking for insurance guides?

Digby Bodenham
1 February 2023 - 1 min read

You might have guessed from our name but we no longer provide guides to home, car, travel, health or life insurance.

We’re focused on helping pet parents with our award-winning pet insurance policies and our flea, tick and worm plan.

You can get a quote for ManyPets pet insurance or check out our Preventative pet care .

If you are looking for help with other kinds of insurance it’s worth using trusted sites like Which? and MoneySavingExpert. Price comparison sites can help you get quotes quickly but make sure you know which policies best meet your needs and that you’re comparing like-for-like cover.

For health and life insurance you can use sites like ActiveQuote to compare policies and get regulated advice.

We hope you find the information you’re looking for.

And if you have a pet, don’t forget to consider our cover. We offer up to £15,000 of lifetime vet fee cover, have 24/7 digital vet support and can cover pre-existing conditions.
