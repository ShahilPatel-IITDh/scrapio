BabySwap Intro

Baby Meta-Finance is a comprehensive and decentralized crypto ecosystem for users to trade, earn, hold, play, and live. It's the best choice for newborn projects on BNB Chain to get support, including AMM, NFT, and GameFi. In the near future, Baby Eco will be innovated and updated to the next version with cutting-edge and strategic features to perfect your journey.
Baby is the future.
Let's go beyond the DEX and build the MetaFi.

As a Trader

You will find potential baby projects on BabySwap early and accompany them to grow up into 'rock stars' through trading, farming, etc.

As a Project

You will find the best support on BabySwap, including growth fund, arbitrage support, entertaining activities, resource connection, friendly display, etc.

As a Holder

You will find the most effortless and effective way on BabySwap to hold to earn lucratively by simply being a landholder on Baby Wonderland, vBABY holder, etc.

As a BUIDLer

You will be taking a huge part in the entire Baby Ecosystem governance to build the Baby Meta-Finance soon with Baby DAO.

DeFi

  • Swap instantly, on-chain, and non-custodial for all BEP 20 tokens on BabySwap.
  • Earn passive income by adding your tokens to liquidity pools.
  • Deposit any assets or Liquidity Pool (LP) token to earn from protocol revenues or earn BABY.

Metaverse

  • A comprehensive NFT ecosystem with all Baby NFT Collections, Stake Mechanisms, Profile Systems, Marketplace, Toy Figures, etc.
  • A profitable and thorough Baby Wonderland is the feature and the basis of Babyverse for all users to hold to earn effortlessly and effectively with extensive surprising features.

Platform

  • Find all possible, exciting, and bountiful ways to invest, meet potential projects with all kinds of DeFi services, use BABY to play in our partners home, and claim your deserved rewards.
