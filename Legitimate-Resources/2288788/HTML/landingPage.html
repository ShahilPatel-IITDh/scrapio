    Useful things to know

    • Only the main cardholder can activate cards, including those issued for additional cardholders.
    • You dont need to activate replacement cards if theres no sticker on the front, theres nothing you need to do.
    • If youve forgotten your PIN, you can get an instant reminder in the MBNA Mobile App .
    • You can activate your MBNA credit card online , all you need is your card and some personal details.
