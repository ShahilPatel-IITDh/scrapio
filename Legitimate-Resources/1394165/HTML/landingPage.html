How to Choose Best Hairstyles

Before choosing any hairstyle, be it long, short or medium length, you need to take a close look at your face shape ad features. Your hairstyle also depends on the type of hair you have and also your personality matters. Many people make the mistake of not taking their unique personality into consideration while they choose hairstyles and this result in the hairstyles that they are not satisfied with.

Find your Faceshape Type :

With the help of an Alice Band and the mirror, you will be able to find the shape of your face. Once you know the shape you can find best hairstyles that will suit you face type.

    • Keep the mirror in front of you and hold your hair tight with the Alice band.
    • Use your lipstick to draw the outline of your face in the mirror. Move from the mirror and see the shape.
    • Below you can find few black shapes which will help you to find the shape that best fits you

Best Hairstyles for OVAL Faceshape:

Any style will suit you. If your hair is thick, long you can curl it and cut it which will reduce the thickness. You can now sweep the top part to get a romantic style. If your hair is short, a messy look will be more ideal. You can give your hair a shine to make it look sexy. Now if you don’t want to have curls, you can do a messy crop and make it short.

  • If your hair is straight, make it into layers. This will add volume to your hair and looks appealing too. You can cut your hair short too as it will look best for people who have oval shapes.
  • If your hair is medium texture, you can try a razor cut, which will make you look hot. You can do it on your own. A bob which is looks short at the back and long at the sides will simple look great.
  • Fringe options will suit for the ones who have thick hair. You can try long fringe options if you hair is straight.
  • Again lighting your hair around your face will make your oval shape stand out.

Things to avoid:
Avoid heavy bangs which will end up hiding your perfect features. Don’t wear too much hair around you face as this will lose the shape of your face.
Celebrities include Cindy Crawford, Mariah Carey, Cameron Diaz, Sharon Stone, Tyra Banks, Uma Thurman, and Julia Roberts.

Best Hairstyles for SQUARE Faceshape:

The options for square shape faces are quite sexy. You can choose the style that will be soft near your ears feathered on the top which will be an easy disguise for the people with square jaw. If any of your stylist has suggested a cut that will be above the jawline, do not go for it as it will only increase the square shape of your face.

  • Straight hair? You can choose the poker-straight cut which will be flattering and perfect for the ones with wide jaw.
  • Curly hair? If your hair is curly you can coax textured layers around the face to make your features look strong.
  • How about trying fringe options: you can try fringe options to almost all hair types, except the ones that are curly. Try the one that will touch your eyebrow which will be layered on the sides. In case your hair is frizzy or thick, you can try layering your fringe so that it looks thin. You can make out in the way Victoria Beckham’s hair style how the hair sweeps on her forehead and eyes.
  • Lighting the hair: try coloring the hair that falls around your face which will make your square face look soft.

Things to avoid
You should avoid long straight styles which will make your square jaw look prominent. A layered bob will suit rather than having a straight bob which will not suit your face type. Layered bob should be below the jaw line or it should end above. The style whatever you choose should give some round shape to the same.
Famous personalities with square shape face are Demi Moore, Sandra Bullock, Thomas Holly, Kristin Scott, and Isabella Rossellini

Best Hairstyles for HEART Faceshape:

If you have a heart shape face, it is better to avoid heavy cuts.
Make sure the style you choose will make your hair look volume in the bottom of your face. It is better not to have your hair long just few inches up to your shoulder level will do.

  • If you hair is thick and long, try to give soft layers around your chin, and make the layers close to the jaw line so that it doesn’t fall on the face.
  • If your hair is long, curly looks will make your heart-shaped face stand out.
  • Try the Short Bob which is the best style for you. This will look perfect on a wavy hair by giving a choppy cut around the chin.
  • For fringe options make it short if the hair type is thick.
  • Coloring the hair: try highlighting the hair near to the ears to make your face look wider.

Things to avoid:
Short hair for upper face may not look good. Don’t make your crown look too height as this will make your chin look narrow and longer.
Heart face celebrities are: Ashley Judd, Claudia Schiffer, Juliette Bionche, and Naomi Campbe

Best Hairstyles for LOGN or OBLONGFaceshape:

People with long face also have lots of style options.

  • Long straight hair? The perfect way is to go for short cut to make your hair look thick and face wide. You can also have an unstructured. If you are looking for a neat look, try a lengthy cut which will give a glamorous look.
  • Thick hair? Razor cut, will be an ideal option.
  • Fringe options: not much is available for you, but you can have blunt-cut fringe which will hide your long forehead and give a feminine look. If your forehead is small clear the fringe to make it visible.
  • Tips to color your hair: To make your face look shorter, color your hair on the sides of your face and darken your hair on the top.

Things to avoid:
Avoid having long hair which will make you face even longer. It is better having shoulder length hair.
Celebrities include: Kristie Alley, Gabrielle Reece, and Stephanie Seymour

Best Hairstyles for ROUND Faceshape:

Any hair style will suit round face.

  • Hair is curly and long? You can go for choppy layers at the bottom of the hair to give a better shape. Trying wave curl will give a bubbly look.
  • If the hair is straight? For people who have round face, lengthier piece of hair around the face will give a slimmer look and structure the cheekbones.
  • Try fringe options: having a short fringe will make your face look large. A long wispy fringe will suit your hair type and face. If you have a thick hair, try long fringe which will make your face look longer.
  • Coloring the hair: coloring or lighting it at the top of your head and side will give a sexy look

Things to avoid:
Don’t go for short crops or straight bangs. Having a round cut which ends at the chin will make you face look big.  As the face will be wide near to the cheeks and ears so avoid cutting it fully to make it look even wider.
Celebrities include: Kate Winslet, Drew Barrymore, Charlotte Church, and Roseanne Barr.
