NABA greensboro Member testimonials

We strive to create a stellar experience for you as a participant in our chapter. Read more about the experience of a few of our members below. Contact us today or visit the “Join Us” section to learn more and get involved.

Melicia james, young professional

“NABA Greensboro’s support and guidance throughout my professional development process has been very instrumental. While serving as the president of ALOBEAEM, North Carolina A&T’s student chapter of NABA, I was able to grow both professionally and personally based on the connections made within the organization. Its leadership team and members invest in students and their dreams. They create an environment that yields success and have embodied the motto of lifting as we climb.”

Teresa Walker, CPA

“NABA Greensboro has added so much value to my life from multiple perspectives.  First and far most, the organization provides me with a networking opportunity to share ideas with like-minded professionals.  Secondly, being in academia, NABA provides me with the opportunity to expose future business leaders to “next level” activities.  Thirdly, being involved with the planning of some of our signature events, such as the Scholarship Banquet and the Women of NABA Brunch provides me with the opportunity to work side-by-side with great visionaries.”

Xavier Thomas, Student member

“I can truly say that NABA Greensboro has played a significant role in the development of my business professionalism and self-confidence. It has taught me how to write a resume, conduct myself in an interview, and network. It has also provided me with the necessary tools in order to be successful, scholarships to fund my education, connections and internship/job opportunities. NABA Greensboro is not just an organization that I’m apart of, but it’s a family. Thanks for everything NABA Greensboro.”
