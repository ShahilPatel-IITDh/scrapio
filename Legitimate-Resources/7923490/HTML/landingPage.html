    About

    Description

    Surguja district is a district of the Indian state of Chhattisgarh. The district is one of the oldest districts of Chhattisgarh. The headquarters of the district is Ambikapur. The district lies in its eponymous Surguja dialectal region and is to the east of the Vindhyachal-Baghelkhand region of peninsular India. Wikipedia
