More about Zoch Verlag

Zoch games – a must-have for game fans.

Whether you want to have fun with your children or spend an entertaining evening with friends, Zoch ’s extensive range of original games offers something for all occasions. With a large choice of board, card and dice games, there is something to suit all tastes.

With Zoch , fun, action and innovation are guaranteed. The games are equally popular among children and adults and feature loving and creative designs. The most popular games include "Spinderella", "Geistesblitz", "Da ist der Wurm drin", "Zicke Zacke Hühnerkacke" and "Heckmeck am Bratwurmeck". The games are designed to improve responsiveness and memory and require tactical skills.

“Spiel des Jahres” award: Zoch games – award-winning games with decades of experience

Zoch has been making fun and creative games for over 30 years. The board, card and dice games are not just popular in Germany – they are also sold in 19 other countries and rank among the bestselling games.

Zoch’s games have received numerous awards, including “Kinderspiel des Jahres" (“Children’s game of the year”) and “Deutscher Kinderspielepreis” (“German children’s game prize). Family games such as “Villa Palletti” and “Niagara” have received the renowned “Spiel des Jahres” (“Game of the year”) award.

Premium toys designed for children

Quality is the utmost priority during production. All products are made from harmless and high-quality materials, and the vast majority of products are made in Germany.

Wood is a popular material that is used in many games. The renewable material is easy to handle and has a long lifespan.

In order ensure that Zoch games make a contribution to environmental protection, most products are made from recyclable materials using cutting-edge manufacturing techniques. As the majority of products are made in Germany, Zoch is able to guarantee that its games comply with German regulations. Internal procedures are also formulated according to the highest quality standards.
