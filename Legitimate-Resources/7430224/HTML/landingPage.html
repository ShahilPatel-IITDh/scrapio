Featured product

SeatSaver® Designed to fit Tesla - Black

£54.99

Protect your Tesla’s vegan leather upholstery from wear and tear inflicted by children and their car seats.

Prince Lionheart’s SeatSaver® technology has been used by millions of families around the world. The high-density construction of the original 2-Stage SeatSaver® has been applied to fit the Tesla seats perfectly. The design ensures that cinched down seats and hard edges don't permeate or over-stretch the Tesla vegan leather used in all models.

The SeatSaver® has a non-slip backing on the bottom panel to ensure it stays in place for maximum protection and is designed to continue to work as your child grows, with multiple protection features for both rear and front-facing seat and boosters.

  • PERFECT FIT – Designed to fit like a glove and complement the unique design of Tesla seats, including the distinctly shaped headrest.
  • ORIGINAL & BEST – Technology taken from the #1 selling car seat protector worldwide, recommended by car buffs as the ultimate car seat protector, applied to perfectly fit Tesla seats in Models S, 3, X & Y.
  • ISOFIX & i-Size COMPATIBLE- Works with all car and booster seats, rear and front-facing options, including belt-based child restraint systems.
  • SUPERIOR PROTECTION - High-density foam protects car upholstery from depression and damage caused by children's car seats and ISOFIX/i-Size child restraint systems, a significant issue associated with Tesla’s vegan leather seats.
  • ULTIMATE PROTECTION – Patented design includes a lip barrier to contain inevitable crumbs and spills, and double kick guard to protect your car from dirty little feet in both front and rear facing car seat positions.
  • GRIPS - Non-slip bottom tray keeps the car seat in the proper position.
