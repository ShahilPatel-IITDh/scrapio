Expert Checking Service

Once you have fully prepared your visa applications and are set to submit. Please contact us and we will provide a checking service. We can meet personally, or you can send your application to our office. Our Expert Checking Service process will be as follows:

  • Call our Adelaide office by making use of the links on this page or scheduling a call back request at the top of this page, and our migration agents will call you back instead.
  • Our migration agents will agree on a time and date for the Checking Service meeting, and we can explain in detail how the migration service will work.
  • You have the choice to pay a visit to our office in Adelaide with your fully prepared visa applications and meet with our migration agent Adelaide in-person, or you can get us e-copies of the documents and forms, and we will provide you the service over Skype.
  • During this time, our agents will thoroughly evaluate all the answers you entered during the online application, all the documents you think are required to secure the visa approval and guide you through all you need to know to stand a chance of getting approved.
  • Our migration agents will guide you through all the changes required to your answers on the cover letter and form, and then guide you of the missing documents you have to submit in order to ensure your application is successful.
  • Please bear in mind that you really should fill out the online form, and therefore do not submit it to allow us to make any required changes. When you send an online form, be advised that no changes can be made if you have made an error.
  • Even when you are preparing for your visa applications, we suggest making a list of all the questions, queries, and concerns that you may have and get the most out of our assistance.
  • The advice is general advice only as we do not have total control of the lodgement process and will not be providing legal submissions.
