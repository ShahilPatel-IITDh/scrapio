Improving your customer experience with PayPal
There are some simple changes you can make to improve your customer experience and help reduce the number of disputes and chargebacks you receive.
TIP 1.
Ensure your business name correctly appears on your customers' credit card statement to avoid any confusion about what the charge relates too.
Update your business name now
TIP 2.
Include your contact details in the message service section of your PayPal account so customers are able to get in touch with you directly, before choosing to file a chargeback.
