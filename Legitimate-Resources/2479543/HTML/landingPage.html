All Feature on Rajakerja Can You Get.

The following are features available on rajakerja.com that you can use as job seekers and employers to meet your needs in the recruitment process.

Talent Sourcing

We invite job seekers who need the opportunity for a career and improve their technical skills and find it easy to get to know companies that need the skills you have today.

Employer Branding

We promote job seekers who need the opportunity for a career and showcase their technical abilities as well as find it easy to be recognized by companies that need workers with criteria like yours.

Tracking System

Rajakerja presents a tracking system that makes it easy for employer companies to find out which candidates are suitable according to the criteria for the job being offered.

Online Test

We provide important features in the recruitment process, namely online assessments using online tests ranging from Intelligence Test, Personality Test, English Test and Test for Technical Skills that can be customized according to employer needs.

Online Interviews

Rajakerja provides an online interview feature that can provide communication solutions between prospective employees and employers through interviews dashboard, during interviews employees can provide notes and interview results using the interview tool that we provide.

Realtime Reports & Announcement

Rajakerja provides a breakthrough in conveying information directly to job seekers and employers through automatic messages via WhatsApp which is a tool for job seekers to get information on recruitment results.

Job List Opportunity

Rajakerja provides information that can provide complete information about job opportunities provided by companies and manufacturers in industrial and commercial areas.
