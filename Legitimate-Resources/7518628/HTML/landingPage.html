The user friendly STS App offers a one-stop solution to all your school transport needs, in the palm of your hand. It’s loaded with features that will make your school bus experience Safe, Timely, Smart!

• Parents can login to the STS App with a fingerprint and facial recognition.

• The App Dashboard has an intuitive design that displays your child’s bus details, alerts and notifications, and real time information on the bus’s location.

• The dedicated Track feature allows parents to see the bus’s departure time and estimated time of arrival (ETA). The STS App will also notify parents of any delays.

• Parents can raise service requests and notify the transport team of any changes to their child’s pick-up and drop-off arrangements.

• The STS App provides an easy platform for making payments and accessing payment history. Google Pay and Apple Pay options are now available in addition to the existing card payment option.

• Parents can access more information on STS Group, recent news, offers and promotions.
