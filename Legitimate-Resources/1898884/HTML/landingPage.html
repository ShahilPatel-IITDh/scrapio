Welcome

Welcome To Boonville, North Carolina.

A small town with a big heart

Boonville is set against the backdrop of the Yadkin Valley Wine Region. Here, you will remember how to be carefree as you savor our delicious wine and beautiful rural landscape.

Whether you are in the mood for a relaxing picnic in the vineyard or an exhilarating zipline through the tree canopy, a family trip to the blueberry farm or a romantic dinner for two, we invite you to try it all. So, come get lost in the little moments that only happen here.
