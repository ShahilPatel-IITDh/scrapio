PANEL STRUCTURES

South-Pak offers two distinct branded product lines to fit the needs and budgets of a variety of industries

PLASTI-CLAD™

Constructed from South-Pak's proprietary engineered Bi-Corr™ panels

Features:

  • 25-35% lighter than comparable wood cases
  • Very high strength to weight ratio
  • No mold, water damage, or import/export issues
  • Higher puncture resistance than wood
  • Best in class hardware and double angle extrusions

ROAD RAGE™

ATA compliant hardware and wood panels built in-house to your specifications

Features:

  • 9 or 12mm high-quality Luan™ wood panels
  • Chemically bonded ABS outer laminate
  • Best in class hardware and double angle extrusions
  • Fully ATA compliant
  • Competitively prices, fully warranteed
