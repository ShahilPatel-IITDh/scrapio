We design and manufacture composite piping solutions for use across the Oil & Gas, Industrial, Water, and Marine & Offshore Industries.

Our solutions include piping system design, manufacturing, engineering services, project management, technical support, field supervision, and training.

Sectors

Composite piping solutions create value for applications across a wide range of industry sectors.

Oil & Gas

Composite pipes offer excellent resistance against corrosive environments including soils, hydrocarbons, salt water, H2S, and chemical compounds typically encountered in any oil field. Composite pipes are exceptionally durable, making them an excellent choice as they create value in a world of ever-increasing energy demand.

Industrial

Composite pipes accommodate a variety of assembly options and complex configurations in the Industrial sector due to their versatility and capacity to withstand high pressures, temperatures, and loads, as well as their excellent chemical resistance.

Water

Safe, reliable, and long-lasting makes composite pipes a natural choice for the water industry. They provide a better hydraulic performance than steel, ductile iron, and concrete, meaning projects can benefit from significantly reduced Operating Expenses.

Marine & Offshore

Onshore projects are becoming more demanding for higher sizes and pressures leading to bulkier products. As offshore exploration goes into deeper water, platform weights become prohibitive.Composite pipes are 75% lighter than steel, by deploying of composites in as many service applications as possible, weights can be reduced, allowing for faster installation and the use of lighter construction equipment.

Solutions

Our extensive portfolio of products combined with a wide range of solutions allow us to offer a comprehensive piping solution.

Manufacturing

We design and manufacture composite piping systems, selecting the best product for your needs, manufacturing pipes, fittings, and prefabricating spools.

Engineering Services

Future Pipe Industries offers optimized pipe systems design and engineering, including stress and surge analysis as well as pipe support packages and detailed engineering.

Project Management

Future Pipe Industries participates and assists in your project execution from start-up to completion ensuring that your project is managed efficiently.

Field Services

To ensure your installation is executed flawlessly we work with you to oversee every aspect of your project site activities. We offer a full range of professional field assistance services including training and bonder certification of your team.
