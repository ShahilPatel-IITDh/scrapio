Nyamazana Safaris

Welcome to Nyamazana Safaris

Where you will enjoy a memorable safari that will surpass your expectations with Professional Hunter & Outfitter Wayne Van Den Bergh. Look no further for big game hunting in Zimbabwe.

“NYAMAZANA” translated from Ndebele means ‘Wild Animals’. That is what we strive to deliver, to hunt where the Game that you pursue is truly WILD.

We are not limited to only one area but several great hunting zones around Zimbabwe, where a large number of different species can be made available for you. Your hunt will be structured to facilitate your needs. We endeavour to make your hunt as memorable as possible to give you a life time of many great memories.

We have secured hunting blocks specializing mainly in affordable Elephant hunts, apart from the Plains Game, Leopard and Crocodile – all free range. We have also built very comfortable bush camps in 3 locations to maximize the usage of the hunting available, as wellas having 3 new boreholes drilled – with this there is now extra water being made available to all the game. It is our intention to drill more boreholes and develop this area even further in the years to come.

Apart from that, we have acquired great Leopard hunting South of Bulawayo in the  Matopos region, as well as other areas north of Bulawayo, with great accommodation and plains game available.

In addition, Nyamazana Safaris has secured long term leases to properties adjacent to Bubiana Conservancy primarily for leopard, as well as additional plains game, still hunting on and from Bubiana Conservancy. This area has a lot to offer for a fair price.

All open and free range hunting.
