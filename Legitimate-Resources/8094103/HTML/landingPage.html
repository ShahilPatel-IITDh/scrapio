When Trace Scott gets the opportunity to leave New York and do an article in her hometown, she doesn't hesitate.  As an investigative reporter she thinks she has covered everything, but not what she's about to incur.  She's tracking a story on an eccentric, retired Inventor.  Black and white soon become gray on this walking enigma.  Quickly the lines of professionalism are blurred, as she is pulled into his infectious personality.  Has she found herself falling for a prolific killer, or is there something more sinister in play in the sleepy town?

About the Author
Dan Andersen

_________
An entrepreneur-turned-author after 40 years of business experience, including a patent in aviation.  Now he has turned his focus to writing.  Andersen has a passion for family, fast cars, comedy and music.  He is truly an anomaly with his ability to persevere.  He has never been scared to enter uncharted waters.  His diverse, eclectic knowledge will take you places where other writers rarely venture.  He writes raw, straight-from-the-heart, gloves-off approach to reach his readers.
