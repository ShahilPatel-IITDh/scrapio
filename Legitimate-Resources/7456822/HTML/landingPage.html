Flexible Loan Programs

Capital Farm Credit is the expert in land loans for rural Texas and has been for more than 100 years. We cater to agricultural producers, agribusiness firms, and recreational property homeowners across the lone star state. As a land loan lender with over 63 locations serving 192 counties across Texas, we work directly with ranchers, farmers, and homeowners looking to purchase land in Texas, turning your dreams into a reality. Our flexible land loans offer competitive rates, flexible terms, and down payment options. Capital Farm Credit's focus is always on you and delivering the best in agricultural land loan lending.
