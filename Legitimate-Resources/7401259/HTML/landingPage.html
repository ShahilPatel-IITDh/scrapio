Live Classes (Classroom & Virtual)
Whatever part of the insurance industry you are involved in, whether a Carrier Adjuster, Public Adjuster or Contractor, knowing and excelling at Xactimate is an integral part of your career. Xactimate is used by a large majority of the carriers so “speaking their language” when submitting estimates is integral. Carrier Adjusters who excel in Xactimate will get hired faster and Public Adjusters and Contractors who excel in Xactimate will get paid faster.

At Crest LLC, we offer the best available training in Xactimate ( XM8 ) for Contractors, Adjusters and anyone involved in the insurance industry. Our classes are taught using Xactimate’s preferred method for higher retention but also put a main focus on passing the Xactimate certification tests. We offer 3 different training options.

​ OPTION 1 - In Person Classroom training
​We offer Xactimate training ( XM8 ) for beginners through intermediate users in person as classroom training (for more information, click here ).

OPTION 2 - Virtual Training

We also offer Level 1 in Spanish.
