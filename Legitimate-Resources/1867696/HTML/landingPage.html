My Day: your tracking home base

When you start your journey, your main focus will be tracking food. Studies show that those who consistently track what they eat tend to lose the most weight.

Your My Day dashboard also allows you to track activity and other key metrics of your weight-loss journey. Keeping a record of these factors helps you see what's impacting your success over time.

Activity tracking

Regular activity can lead to more weight loss than changing your diet alone. We help you ease into exerciseand have fun doing it.

Food

Easily find the Points value for foods and drinks, then track what you eat with just a couple taps.

Weight

Tracking food isnt the only predictor of success; weighing yourself regularly also helps you reach your goals.

Healthy-eating zone

A blue dot means youve tracked and stayed within a healthy Points range that day.
