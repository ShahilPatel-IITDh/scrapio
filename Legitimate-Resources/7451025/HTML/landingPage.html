Your roadmap to wellness

Repsol goal is to promote and educate our employees on the importance of healthy/wellness living.  With that in mind, we introduce you to the Repsol Wellness Program.  This wellness program provides you access to wellness resources to help you find ways to bring health and wellness into focus.  It applies to all our employees as well as their spouses covered by the medical plan.

Repsol Wellness Program has several resources to promote healthy living.

  • Group & individual Challenges
  • Fitness Videos
  • Meal Planning
  • Healthy Recipes
  • Biometric Screening
  • Rewards and much more

The GoodRx app is a useful tool to help you find the best deals for your prescription drugs in your area.

Keep in mind that GoodRx shows the cash price and does not reflect your particular plan’s benefits. Remember to verify that any pharmacy you find using GoodRx is in your plan’s network.

Employee assistance program

800-964-3577
Group #: GLT-872988
www.guidanceresources.com


The EAP is available to all active employees covered under the LTD program. Ability Assist EAP can help you 24/7 with confidential support, guidance and resources.

  • Toll-free phone and web access 24/7
  • Phone access to legal counsel
  • Work/life services for assistance with:
  • Child care, elder care and adoption
  • Relationships
  • Financial issues
  • Stress

Visit guidanceresources.com to create your own personal user name and password. As a first-time user, you’ll be asked to provide the following information on the profile page:

  1. In the Company/Organization field, use: HLF902 Then, create your own confidential user name and password
  2. Finally, in the Company Name field at the bottom of the personalization page, use: ABILI (all caps)
