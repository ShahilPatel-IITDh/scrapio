  • Daiquiri Palace

    Daiquiri Palace is a unique bar/restaurant operating in the Balearic Islands of Spain. Daiquiri Palace has been a family run business since 1986 and is situated in the center of the ever inspiring Magaluf beach, the Daiquiri Palace is going from strength to strength in providing the tastiest frozen cocktails in the Mediterranean.     A family owned business since 1986, the Daiquiri phenomenon was created in New Orleans, but bringing it to Europe was the families idea! The Daiquiri Palace Way

In Everything we do, we inspire to make your holiday visit to Calvia Beach resort a pleasurable and memorable experience.

The way we make this happen is by having a first-class staff, a comfortable seating area, and a sound system playing the tunes you like, plenty of laughter, smiles and relaxing with your friends.

We also provide the best Daiquiris on the island! Along with fantastic cocktails, ice cold beer, nutritional smoothies, world famous burgers, homemade chips and pizzas. We can not forget the delicious ice cream and crepes!

We are looking forward to your visit!
