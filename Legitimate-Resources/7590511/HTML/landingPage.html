Tokenomics

By optimizing the two tokens ($THC & $THG), Thetan Arena aims at the ecosystem's sustainability and scalability.

The two tokens serve as economic incentives for players to participate in a variety of activities in Thetan Arena. Such mechanics serves major purposes:
- For players to take home the rewards, utilize the tokens, thus creating an internal circulating economy
- To decentralize the ownership of digital assets and network governance of Thetan Arena
