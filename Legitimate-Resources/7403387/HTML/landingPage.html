Money Grows on Trees Online Slots

They say that money doesn’t grow on trees, but mFortune Casino is out to prove that old adage wrong! Money Grows on Trees mobile slots is set in an enchanted forest where the branches of the trees are heavy with prizes, ripe for the picking.

Locked to 20 win lines, this fruity mobile slots game is full of quirky features that can deliver a windfall! Find enough Plant Food to access Super Coins Free Spins mode and watch those winnings grow. To play Money Grows on Trees, you’ll need a desktop or laptop PC, or a device running iOS 10 or Android 5.0 and above.

How to Play Money Grows on Trees

As Money Grows on Trees is locked to 20 win lines, all you need to do is press ‘Bet’ to decide your bet-per-line. Use the ‘+’ and ‘-’ icons to increase or decrease your bet amount, which can be anything from 1p up to £5 per line. This will be multiplied by 20, for all 20 win lines, to determine your total bet. This will adjust automatically as you alter your settings.

To give you an example, if you chose a bet-per-line of 10p, this would be multiplied by 20 to give you a total bet of £2.

Reel Wins

Wins from the money tree will be a multiplier of the bet-per-line you just set up. The value of that multiplier varies depending on which symbols you’ve lined up and how many of them. You will need at least three symbols in a row on a win line to bear fruit.

For more details on the winning combinations you can cultivate, tap the menu button and select ‘Paytable’. All wins pay from left to right across the reels, and winning lines will be highlighted to show you where you have a win. Any prizes you pluck from the tree are added to your credit automatically.

Only the highest-value win on each win line will count, but you can win across multiple win lines with a single spin. If that happens, all of those prizes will be added together to form your windfall total win.

In Money Grows on Trees, you’ll see coins sprouting from the branches of the money tree. Each coin has a multiplier on it, and these will change each time you spin. If you spin in three coin symbols anywhere on the reels at once, you’ll win all of the coins currently hanging from the money tree, as well as any on-reel wins.

Wild Symbols

Look out for the Wild mushrooms in this one! They can be very handy indeed, as they’ll substitute for almost every other symbol on the reels to help you score a win. The only symbols they can’t stand in for is the Coins symbol and Plant Food free spins.

Let’s say you spun in two juicy watermelon symbols on a win line with a Wild alongside. That would earn you the same multiplier as if you’d lined up three watermelon symbols. Sweet!

If you try to win the progressive Jackpot with the help of Wild symbols, unfortunately you’ll be barking up the wrong tree! The only way to win the progressive Jackpot is with five golden apple Jackpot symbols on one win line, but a combination of Wilds and Jackpot symbols will still yield a juicy prize.

Free Spins

When you find yourself a money tree, you’ll want to keep it well fed! That’s why the Plant Food symbols are so important. If you spin in three or more of them anywhere on the reels, you’ll activate Super Coins Free Spins mode!

Getting 3, 4, or 5 Plant Food symbols will award 5, 10, or 15 free spins respectively, locked to the bet settings of your last paid-for spin. Extra coins will begin to sprout from the tree, offering even bigger multipliers than you’ll see in normal play.

If you spin in three Plant Food symbols while in Super Coin Free Spins mode, you’ll activate an extra level called Platinum Spin! All of the coins on the tree will upgrade to the maximum possible multipliers for ONE spin only. If you manage to spin in three Coin symbols with this single Platinum Spin, you’ll win ALL of the massive multipliers on the tree. Either way, after your Platinum Spin has been used, you’ll return to regular free spins mode.

Progressive Jackpot

The occurrence of 5 (five) “JACKPOT” Jackpot symbols on an active win line awards a progressive Jackpot. The progressive Jackpot is available on any bet, with 0.005% of the bet being added to the progressive pot. There is no hidden reserve pot.

When the Jackpot is won, it is reset and begins to rise again. Simultaneous wins can occur, if another player wins the progressive Jackpot then a notification to this effect would occur BEFORE the current player was awarded the Jackpot.

The game Jackpot seed value is £2000, The Jackpot is individual for this game, it is not shared with any other games.

In the event of a communication error after a progressive win result has been determined and before it has been displayed on screen, the win amount will automatically be transferred to the player’s account. While the game is live, the Jackpots cannot be terminated.
