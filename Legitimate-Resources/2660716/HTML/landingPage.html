Welcome
"Windsong" AAEA Founders Award for Oil
Pamela Wildermuth has been drawing and painting horses for as long as she can remember.  For those of you who share her appreciation for this beautiful animal, please enjoy your visit.

Maybe you are interested in purchasing an original work of art, or perhaps a print.  Maybe you would like to commission a portrait of that special horse, either for yourself or as a gift.  Or maybe you just want to look.  Welcome.

"Racin' the Weather"
"About to Bolt"
"Daughters of the Wind"
"My work is not only inspired by the beauty and grace of the horse, it is influenced by the unique relationship between man and horse.  Unlike our other close animal companions, dogs and cats, the horse is not a fellow predator, but a prey animal.  That they have come to trust, work with, and bond with man is an amazing testament to their intelligence and adaptability." Pamela Wildermuth
