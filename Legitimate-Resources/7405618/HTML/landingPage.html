Audit Clients

Obtaining audit confirmations from third parties can be a tricky and time-consuming process for your auditors. Non-responses are common, creating additional work for auditors, and causing delays to audit completion. This is especially concerning when reporting deadlines are tight.

Confirmation.com's online platform helps auditors to perform audit confirmations more effectively with their client's constituents such as banks, law firms, customers, and suppliers. This ensures that auditors obtain responses from validated institutions and authorized responders, which combats potential confirmation fraud.

Providing authorization to disclose information

Audit clients are required to provide their authorization to the confirming party to disclose information to their auditor as part of the confirmation process. Historically a paper process, Confirmation.com has a quick and simple digital authorization process.
