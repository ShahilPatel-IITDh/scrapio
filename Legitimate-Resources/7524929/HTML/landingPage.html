Caution, fraudsters

How to protect yourself:

Do not install remote access apps e.g. AnyDesk, TeamViewer) on your devices under any circumstances, even if you are supposedly asked to do so by UBS.

End suspicious phone calls immediately, especially if you are urged to grant access to your computer or to confirm a payment or transaction that you were not expecting and that you do not recognize.

Fraudsters are increasingly appearing as buyers on classified ad platforms . To get a payment as a seller, never share your E-Banking contract number or the three-digit security code (CVV) of your credit card, and never use the Access App to scan QR codes outside the UBS E-Banking login page.
