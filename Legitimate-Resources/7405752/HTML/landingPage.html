How long will my tree removal take?
It's a common question asked of tree removal professionals: How long does it take to remove a large tree? The answer, unfortunately, is not as cut and dry as one might hope. Here are a few factors that will affect the timeline of large tree removal:

The first factor is the size of the tree. A small tree will obviously take less time to remove than a large one. But just how big is "large?" That's tough to say, as it depends on the equipment being used and the experience of the workers. Generally speaking, though, you can expect a large tree removal to take anywhere from several hours to an entire day.
The second factor is the location of the tree. If the tree is in a difficult-to-reach spot, it will naturally take longer to remove.

What to do if a tree is too close to my house?
If your tree is too close to your house, you may need to remove or trim it. Here are some things to consider when deciding whether to remove or trim your tree:
1. The size of the tree. If the tree is small, you may be able to trim it yourself. If the tree is large, you may need to hire a professional to remove it.
2. The type of tree. Some trees are easier to trim than others. You may want to consult with a professional before attempting to trim a difficult tree yourself.
3. The location of the tree. If the tree is close to power lines or other structures, you may need to hire a professional to remove it.
