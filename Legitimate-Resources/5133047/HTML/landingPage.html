Refinancing & Equity


Open the door to opportunities.

Maybe you’re looking for money for home improvements or other large purchases. Maybe your goal is to lower your monthly payment or become mortgage free sooner. The good news is our local home loan experts can help unlock the possibilities that the equity in your home can provide you.

Use your equity to your benefit.

You’ve worked hard to build equity in your home. Take advantage of it by:

Paying down your mortgage sooner.

Refinancing to lower your monthly payment.

Getting cash out to use for anything.


You can be mortgage-free sooner.

The Community First 10 & Done Home Loan allows you to tap into your home’s equity or pay off your mortgage faster giving you more freedom. Freedom to retire, freedom to travel, freedom to spend more on all the things you love. Plus, it’s easier and more affordable than you think!

  • No escrow requirements

  • No prepayment penalties

  • Pay less interest with bi-weekly payments

  • Receive our helpful, local service for the life of your loan

Looking for a longer or shorter term?

Choose from additional products with 6-year to 30-year term options.

Home Equity Fixed Rate Loan



Perfect for making a single large purchase or debt consolidation.

  • Low fixed rate locked in for 6 years with the option to amortize payments up to 30 years

  • ​Consistent monthly payments with bi-weekly payment options to save you thousands of dollars in interest

  • Home Equity Loans qualify for loan discounts when you have your checking with us

Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC)



Perfect for ongoing purchases. No annual fee or need to re-apply each time – enjoy easy access to funds for up to 10 years. Choose the method that works for you:

  • Home Equity Line Visa ® Card – Make purchases online or at any retailer that accepts Visa. Need cash? Use your card at any Community First ATM for no fee

  • Traditional – Transfer money as needed using Digital Banking, by calling or visiting a branch
