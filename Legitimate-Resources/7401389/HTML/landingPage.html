When it's time to renovate your kitchen or build a new home, you might want to consider updating your countertops as well. Some of the benefits of installing new countertops include:

Improved Aesthetics

If your countertops look old and drab, it can affect the overall aesthetic of your Ann Arbor kitchen. New countertops are an excellent way to give your kitchen a fresh, new look while also increasing its functionality. If you choose a stunning material like granite or quartz, your kitchen will be the envy of your friends and family! Don't wait to take advantage of this affordable upgrade to your home.

Increased Functionality

If you've been using the same countertops for many years, they may be starting to show signs of wear and tear. Cracks, chips, and stains can make it difficult to prepare meals and snacks, not to mention hard to keep your kitchen clean. A new set of countertops can solve all these problems and more. Enhanced Durability

Countertops take a lot of abuse over the years. Not only do they have to withstand daily use, but they're also constantly exposed to heat and moisture. If your countertops are looking a bit worn out, it might be time for an upgrade. Our countertops are extremely durable and resistant to damage caused by heat or moisture. You can rest assured that they'll stand up to the test of time!

Professional Installation

Ann Arbor Countertops professionals will make sure your new countertops look perfect and fit well in your kitchen space. You can trust that we'll get the job done right, so you won't have to worry about costly errors or faulty workmanship when we're done with the project.

Simple to Clean & Maintain

One of the best features of new countertops is that they're easy to clean and maintain. All you need is a damp cloth and some soap to keep them looking shiny and new. Plus, our countertops are resistant to spills and stains, so you don't have to worry about anything ruining their appearance. When you choose a timeless design like quartz or granite, you can keep your countertops looking sleek and stylish for years to come.

Quartz

Quartz is a man-made material that looks just like natural stone. This unique product comes in a variety of colors and styles, so you're sure to find something that matches your style and needs. Quartz also has a non-porous surface that's easy to clean, which makes it ideal for kitchens and bathrooms.

Granite

Granite is one of the most popular countertop materials on the market today because it's extremely durable and low maintenance. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Install My Countertops Myself?

Some people decide to try installing their own countertops instead of hiring professional installation services. While this is possible in most cases, it's not recommended for most homeowners because it takes extensive knowledge about measurements and proper installation techniques in order to ensure your countertop doesn't warp or bend over time.

Will My New Countertops Have a Seam?

Most countertops have a seam, which can be a hassle to look at and clean. However, our countertops don't have visible seams because we install them with a unique process that makes them seamless. Plus, you can choose from a variety of different finishes to hide the seam, so they appear seamless.

Can You Hook Up My Hardware After Installation?

Yes! We can hook up your hardware after installation, so you're ready to use your new countertops right away. We'll also give you a full tutorial on how to use and care for your new countertops so you can enjoy them for years to come.

Will My New Countertops Scratch Easily?

Most countertops will scratch easily, but our quartz and granite countertops don't scratch as easily because they're man-made materials with a non-porous surface.
