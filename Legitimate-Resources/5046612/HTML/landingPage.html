Our mission

We bring the best of Google to help solve some of humanity’s biggest challenges — combining funding, innovation, and technical expertise to support underserved communities and provide opportunity for everyone.

  • How we’re helping people and cities adapt to extreme heat

    Extreme heat is a problem we can’t tackle alone. We are supporting nonprofit projects that identify which communities are most affected by extreme heat and where there are opportunities to help.

    March 29, 2023

  • Google's Cybersecurity Clinics fund

    In collaboration with the Consortium of Cybersecurity Clinics , we will support selected colleges, universities, and community colleges with up to $1M each to increase access and opportunities for students interested in pursuing careers in cybersecurity.

    June 22, 2023

  • Ad Grants: 20 years of nonprofits helping more and spending less

    Launched 20 years ago, Ad Grants has driven more than 14B clicks to nonprofits’ websites, boosting their ability to recruit volunteers, attract donors, reach the communities they help and advocate for the causes they support.

    March 1, 2023
