Availability and Cost

BRADLEY BEACH OFFICE HOURS:

Tuesday-Saturday, first appointment at 10 a.m., last appointment at 6 p.m., and Sunday-Monday, first appointment at 10 a.m., last appointment at 3:30 p.m.

If you seek an appointment outside those hours, contact me and I’ll see if I can accommodate you.

Fees (all including 6.7 percent New Jersey sales tax):
Incall cost: $60 for an hour, $90 for 90 minutes, $120 for two hours
Outcall cost: $90 for an hour, $120 for 90 minutes, $150 for two hours
Manscaping: $1 a minute, or included as part of a massage session

Available for a flat fee of $100 for an hour session for outcalls in hotels in Atlantic City and at Liberty Newark International Airport
