About Viterra

We buy goods such as grains, pulses, oilseeds, biodiesel, rice, sugar and cotton and transport them to our customers around the world, using our vast global network of storage and transport facilities. Through our processing operations, we transform a variety of raw commodities in to value-added products. We are active in 37 countries around the world and employ over 17,500 people worldwide. We pride ourselves on operating responsibly and focus on the safety, sustainability and quality of all our operations around the world.

Our people are a reflection of our values. They are ambitious, driven, take ownership of their work and understand the importance of conducting business fairly, with simplicity and openness. Our first priority is to offer a safe environment for our people, including third parties, contractors and our local communities.

Interested in becoming part of the Viterra workforce? Our head office in Rotterdam, the Netherlands, has a variety of vacancies which may be of interest to you. Head to the Careers page for information about our current openings and how to apply. Want to know more about Viterra as an organisation? Visit our corporate site .
