WHO WE ARE.

Established in 1938, Halifax & Co pioneered in the art of Hand Embroidery in Pakistan and produced High Quality Hand Embroidered Badges for the British and European Armed Forces.

It is a matter of great pride for us that our products become a part of the uniforms of high officials of organizations like French Navy, Dutch Military, British MOD, Dublin Govt Supplies Agency, KLM Airlines, etc.

With the passage of time the company extended its product range. Today we are capable of manufacturing all types of Uniform Accessories for Military, Police, Airlines, Hospitality and Work Wear Uniforms. Our main markets include Germany, Denmark, Norway, UK and Middle East.
