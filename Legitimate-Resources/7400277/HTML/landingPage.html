By Relentlesshome January 25, 2023 February 6, 2023

Vinyl plank floors are one of the most sensitive and fragile surfaces of floor material. It is vital that you clean it gently to avoid the expensive scratches that come along with it. In this article, we are going to look at the best Shark vacuum cleaner for vinyl plank floors.

Shark has been making vacuum cleaners for many decades now. They have been manufacturing all types of vacuum cleaners. Types of vacuum cleaners include upright vacuums, stick vacuums, canister vacuums, and handheld vacuums. However, vacuum cleaners can also be categorized in terms of what type of surfaces they clean.

For instance, there are vacuum cleaners ideal for hardwood floors, tiles, and carpets. There are also special vacuum cleaners for sensitive surfaces that are susceptible to scratches and tears and one such surface is the vinyl plank surface.

If you use your regular vacuum cleaner for this floor, there will be visible scratches on the floor which will ruin the whole look of your house. This is why it is important to observe the type of floor you have before buying a vacuum cleaner. This luxurious floor needs a soft brush that will gently rub it.

Vinyl plank floors are often easy to clean since they are moisture-resistant. However, they are prone to damage if they are exposed to extreme heat or cold.

They also tend to fade and lose their sparkle as time goes and this is why observing good maintenance is essential for these floors. In the following section, we are going to review the best Shark Vacuum cleaners for the luxurious vinyl plank floors.
