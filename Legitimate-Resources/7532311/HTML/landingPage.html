    Description

    Qubec City sits on the Saint Lawrence River in Canada's mostly French-speaking Qubec province. Dating to 1608, it has a fortified colonial core, Vieux-Qubec and Place Royale, with stone buildings and narrow streets. This area is the site of the towering Chteau Frontenac Hotel and imposing Citadelle of Qubec. The Petit Champlain districts cobblestone streets are lined with bistros and boutiques. Google
