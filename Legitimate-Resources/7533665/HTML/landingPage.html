ABOUT MIDNIGHT WORKOUTS

Eclectic, emotional, raw and cinematic, Midnight Workouts is as comfortable taking you on an emotional journey as making you sweat on the dance floor. The two masterminds got together with one purpose in mind; to connect at a deeper level with like minded souls in the search of  something else, something more.

Anarchic in their creations they go with anything that feels right and are best described as putting in a blender, electronica, disco, deep and a pinch of pop.

Now signed to Mite Music, there's a bright future of releases coming, so watch this space.
