Technomart Investment Advisors is an independent, family operated, highly known firm specializing in customized, separately managed accounts. Joseph Parnes, Founder/President is responsible for supplying the firm’s private and institutional clients with actionable investment advice with a special focus on growth companies and long and short selling strategies.Investment services are available to individuals, trusts, and corporations on a discretionary basis.

Joseph Parnes has been a regular contributor to Forbes.com and Modern Trader Magazine, providing a consistently contrarian and optimistic perspective. His investment theology has also been showcased in a broad array of media, including Bloomberg TV, CNBC, Forbes, Worth, Fortune, Investors’ Business Daily, The Nightly Business Report, and formerly Cnnfn.* Joseph gave a keynote speech to the Financial Markets Law Committee c/o Bank of England and the United Kingdom Supreme Court as an advisory opinion on Short selling.** As part of our educational outreach we have just published a new book by Joseph Parnes “Short Selling for the Long Term”( Wiley, 2020)  which sets forth in more detail the investment strategies pursued by our firm. **

* Technomart and or Joseph Parnes did not provide and or receive compensation for contributions and or media appearances .** Joseph Parnes’ travel and accommodations were provided by the FMLC. Joseph Parnes has a royalty interest with Wiley Publishing.
