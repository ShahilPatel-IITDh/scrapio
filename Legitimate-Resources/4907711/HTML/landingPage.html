Journal of Pharmaceutical and Biomedical Sciences

Frequency: Monthly

Journal Access: Open access, published paper are free to downlaod, read and use it for non commerical purpose.

Journal scope and spectrum: Pharmaceutical sciences, drug invention and development, drug delivery, Clinical medicine, microbiology, infectious diseases , biotechnology and chemistry, Cell Biology, clinical pharmacy, computational Biology, Neuroscience , dental drug delivery science and associated latest technology , biomedical and medical sciences.

Submissions include research, reviews, case reports, interventional trials reports, evidence-based medical research reports, meta-analyses, observational studies, retrospective and prospective studies, and other clinical studies.

International membership and covered by: Index medicus of South-East Asia Region(IMSEAR) and Hinari access.


Repository: All published articles will permanently stored and available in full text (e-version) at Zenodo and abstract at OpenAIRE .
