Delivering a high level of security and data privacy for Anthology clients is foundational to our mission of providing data-informed experiences to the global education community. We prioritize protecting our clients’ data while creating unparalleled intelligent experiences that drive learner success. Our products and solutions are built with security at their core, demonstrating that with privacy by design, data-driven personalization and privacy can go hand in hand. Our clients’ data is their own and it is our responsibility to uphold their trust by protecting it vigilantly and in accordance with global data privacy and security standards.
