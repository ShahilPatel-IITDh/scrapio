Connecting caregivers

Enhance how your team of caregivers communicates and collaborates inside and out of your facilities. You can put data, apps, and tools at their fingertips to deliver an exceptional, efficient care experience.

Benefits

Arm caregivers with tech that enhances communication and collaboration and provides highly secure anytime, virtually anywhere access to clinical and operational data

  • Improve workflows

    The ability to gain highly secure access to data virtually anywhere on any device can make your workforce more effective and efficient. Highly secure messaging enables your employees to communicate and coordinate care between clinicians, patients, as well as access EMR to provide clinical decision support at the point of care.

  • Secure patient information, critical systems, and endpoints

    Comprehensive end-to-end cybersecurity solutions help protect patient information, mission-critical systems, and an ever-growing number of end-points to help your employees more securely communicate and collaborate.

  • Increased visibility into workforce to improve efficiencies

    Locate and collaborate with mobile caregivers using group talk, text, and management tools. Communicate with on-the-go EMT first responders via the dedicated FirstNet network to improve ER workflows.

Changing patient expectations, new technologies and greater competition are transforming the healthcare sector. Find out how the contact center can help you win in this new environment.
