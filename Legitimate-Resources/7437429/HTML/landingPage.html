EA991 DRIVING ACADEMY

EA991 Driving Academy, is proud to be a Botswana Qualifications Authority (BQA) Accredited Driving Academy.

The EA991 Driving Academy, based in Gaborone has been operating since 2020. We offer our customers the opportunity to learn how to safely drive a motor vehicle within a professional environment, with learning facilitated by certified and highly qualified Driver Trainers in accordance with the Botswana Driving Schools and Driving Instructors Regulations.
