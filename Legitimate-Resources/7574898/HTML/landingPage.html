    cinematic
    Learn to pronounce
    adjective
    adjective : cinematic
    1. relating to the cinema.
      "cinematic output"
    noun
    noun : cinematic ; plural noun : cinematics
    1. 1 .
      the visual qualities or aesthetics of a film or video.
      "even if horror-movie cinematics are not to your taste, the show is arresting"
    2. 2 .
      Gaming
      (in a video game) a scene shown to the player when they reach a particular point in the game, such as at the end of a level or when the player's character dies; a cutscene .
      "players must select a chapter, watch a cinematic, win a fight, and move on"
