Tech Flicks

Now Playing & Coming Soon!

  • Dinosaurs of Antarctica

    Journey to the south polar landscapes of Antarctica hundreds of millions of years ago. Join Antarctic scientists on a quest to understand the ice continent's profound transformation, and predict the future as humans drive dramatic change.

  • Serengeti

    Prepare to be awed by this immersive giant screen IMAX® film about how nature works in one of the world’s greatest ecosystems.

  • Wings Over Water

    Wings Over Water tells the story of the epic journeys of three amazing bird families – the Sandhill Crane, the Yellow Warbler and the Mallard Duck – with extraordinary footage of their fascinating behaviors.

  • Everest

    "Everest" is back for the first time in IMAX Laser! See the dramatic true story of a team of climbers who found hope, strength and triumph in the wake of tragedy.

  • Ireland

    A joyful adventure through Ireland following Irish writer Manchán Magan, joined by Irish concert violinist Patricia Treacy and four teen music students who together discover new insight into their Irish traditions and culture.

At our 280-seat IMAX Dome Theater you’ll see, hear and feel more, with the world’s first laser projector for a dome theater, 9,000+ square feet of wraparound image, and 13,000 watts of digital surround sound. The IMAX dome experience with a laser projector is technically advanced and visually stunning, a one-of-a-kind movie experience. Offerings from renowned filmmaking companies like National Geographic and MacGillivray Freeman educate and entertain, with adventure, drama and emotion unfolding via IMAX’s trademark visual clarity and sound quality.

Seating is not assigned in the IMAX Dome Theater. Please arrive early to find your preferred seats.

Be sure to stop by our concession stand for more than a hundred choices of cold Coca-Cola beverages from our Freestyle machine, fresh popcorn with real butter, plus candy and other snacks. Download the Freestyle app to your smartphone and our machine will dispense your custom soda blend!

IMAX for Field Trips

Take your class on a field trip under the sea or into space! Through IMAX technology and the giant screen, journey to places where your students can be inspired without leaving their seats.

School groups qualify for a special rate. Please ask about this opportunity when making your reservations.

See our calendar for regular showing IMAX films. If you are a group of 50 or more, the 10 a.m. and 11 a.m. time slots can be reserved with a film of your choosing from our library . Show times and IMAX films are based upon availability.
