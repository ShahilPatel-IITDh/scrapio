Navy Federal Overseas Auto Buying Program

  • Buy Better Abroad

    With exclusive benefits and pricing on the vehicle you want, Navy Federal Credit Union makes it easier than ever to get a great deal on a new car while you’re stationed overseas or deployed at sea!

  • Eligibility

    Open to Active Duty military, DOD civilians, government contractors and members of the diplomatic community, this program is designed to fit the needs of military personnel currently deployed at sea in international waters or stationed outside the United States on PCS, TDY or other assignment orders of 30 days or more.

    Proof of eligibility is required, and buyers must be authorized to purchase through the Army and Air Force Exchange/Navy Exchange System for on-base orders made for Ford Military Group, Chrysler Military Group and Harley-Davidson.

  • Satisfaction Guarantee

    Backed by a 100% money-back guarantee, you’ll get a full refund if your vehicle isn’t delivered exactly as specified. On top of that, you’re entitled to a Lowest Price Guarantee on vehicles from select manufacturers for stateside delivery.

  • Options & Delivery

    Custom order your vehicle or choose from a wide selection of in-stock options for immediate delivery. Delivery is available almost anywhere in the U.S. or at most overseas duty stations.
