Welcome!

Music Journey uses music to help people reach their potential. We believe in the profound power of music to heal. Our board certified music therapists use music to address the goals of the people we serve, in Pittsburgh and the surrounding area.

Who We Serve

We help people of Pittsburgh and the surrounding areas. Through the processes of music therapy, we work with groups or individually with a person, a family, or an organization to set goals and accomplish them. We have worked with families recovering from domestic violence, adults with relationship difficulties, teenagers with anxiety and depression, as well as adults with depression, anxiety, or histories of trauma. We have helped children with developmental problems and their families. We have also served children and young adults on the Autism Spectrum.
