Hope For Future Foundation is a registered Charity Organisation founded on July 2010.

We work in close collaboration with the people who live in the urban slums, rural villages and local poor community were most of the hands on action and funding is required.

Our Mission

Our goal is to help eradicate poverty by offering small business grants to the less privileged, to help them launch new businesses with the aim to generate more local jobs and stimulate the economy within the community.

We provide Scholarships to support the education of needy, deserving and disadvantaged students in Africa. The Scholarship allows for future educational planning and continuous support. A student is less likely to drop out of school because the support gives them the confidence they need to thrive intellectually

Our Vision

By working together, we can enable vast amounts of students unlock their creative and academic potential. We can help our local communities to break the cycle of poverty and change their lives for the better.
