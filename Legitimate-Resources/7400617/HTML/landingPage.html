Web accessibility.

H&R Block is firmly committed to compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act. As part of this commitment, we continually improve our website to make it as accessible as possible.

For questions or suggestions about improving our website, please contact H&R Block Customer Support at 1-800-472-5625 . To provide feedback regarding H&R Block’s policy of accessibility, call 855-508-0833 .


Screen readers and browsers.

We recommend using the JAWS screen reader and Firefox browser combination for an optimized experience on the H&R Block website.

For JAWS users, please uncheck the Filter Consecutive Duplicate Links checkbox in the Links section of the JAWS Settings center to allow functionality of the subtab navigation.

To increase the size of text and images, adjust your browser settings by hitting the control key (PC users) or command key (Mac users) and the plus key (+) at the same time.
