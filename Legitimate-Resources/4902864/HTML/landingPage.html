Termites

Termites are a growing problem for Ipswich homeowners. These pests cause significant structural damage to homes, compromising their integrity and value. Get a termite inspection!

Cockroaches

Cockroaches are one of the most persistent pests found in homes and commercial buildings in Ipswich. They are not only unsightly but can also spread diseases and trigger allergies.

Ants

Ants are a common pest problem for Ipswich homeowners. These tiny insects quickly multiply and cause a nuassance. They also contaminate food and spread diseases.

Spiders

Spiders can frighten and be an unwanted presence in your home in Ipswich. While some spiders are harmless, others can be venomous and threaten your family's health and safety.

Silverfish

Silverfish are a common pest problem for Ipswich homeowners. They feed on paper products, books, clothing, and wallpaper and can cause significant damage to your property,

Rodents

Rodents, such as mice and rats, can be a major problem for homeowners in Ipswich. These pests can cause significant damage to your property, contaminate food, and spread diseases.

Paper Wasps

Paper wasps are common headache for Ipswich homeowners. Their stings are painful, and their potent venom can cause intense pain, swelling, and redness. Don't get stung!

House Flies

Flies can be a nuisance and a health concern for homeowners in Ipswich. They can carry disease-causing pathogens and contaminate food and surfaces.

African Black Beetles

African black beetles can turn your lawn into a nightmare! As one of the most notorious lawn pests for homeowners, these creepy crawlers can quickly destroy your lawn!

Lawn Grubs

Lawn grubs can be a destructive problem for homeowners, causing significant damage to lawns and landscapes. These pests feed on the grass roots, leaving behind brown and patchy lawns.

Lawn Weeds

Lawn weeds can be a frustrating problem for homeowners, detracting from the beauty and appearance of outdoor landscapes. These pests can quickly spread and take over lawns.

Lawn Diseases

Lawn diseases can be a serious issue, causing damage to lawns and reducing their overall health and appearance. These diseases can spread quickly and require professional intervention.
