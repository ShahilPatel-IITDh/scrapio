Rug Repair and Restoration

Specialist Rug Restoration

Our team of highly skilled craftsmen are ready to deal with all forms of restoration. From minor to major repairs & alteration our craftsmen are on site in our dedicated, purpose built workshop. It is important to have the best possible work space to support the high level of concentration needed as well as a clean working environment.

Working closely with great attention to detail our restoration works are like no other. We cover all manner of rug restoration and offer a no obligation analysis on the best ways to help you with your rug, from partial to full restoration. When skillfully undertaken, restoration and remedial work to handmade rugs and carpets is virtually undetectable. Our team carries out such works, even if there is a large hole in the center of the rug, rest assured we can restore it.

Our list of rug restoration works covered include:

  • Rug Fringes
  • Selvage Restoration
  • Hole, Tear & Slit Repairs
  • Re-Weaving & Re-Piling To Worn Areas
  • Full Restoration Of Rugs that are Worn Or Damaged

We have an unrivaled reputation in for Oriental and Persian carpet and rug repairing and restoration. Using only the very best craftsmen and rug weavers, we specialize in a traditional hand rug repair service for Persian, Afghan, Kilim, Turkish, Silk and Antique rugs, carpets, runners and tapestries. From small repair to a full Persian carpet restoration service, we offer the complete service.

We are noted for the following:

  • Specialist Oriental Rug cleaning
  • Rug re-fringing and securing fraying sides
  • Threadbare area and hole repair
  • Carpet splits and tears repaired
  • Moth rug damage correction – re-weaving and re-building
  • Antique rug and carpet restoration specialists
  • Pet/pest damage correction – including dog and cat urine removal service
  • Carpet moth prevention treatment
  • Non slip/creep rug underlay supply
  • Part exchange option for old rugs and carpets
  • Carpet and rug valuation service
