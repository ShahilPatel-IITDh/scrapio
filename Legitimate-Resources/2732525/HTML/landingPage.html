Reserved Airport Parking for Travels
For more than two decades, The Parking Spot has been the leader in off-site, near airport parking. Making a reservation provides you discounts every time you park. The loyalty program allows you to earn free parking and other benefits. Spot Club members also get access to parking promos and deals throughout the year. Our seamless reservation system eliminates traveler frustrations with parking at the airport.

When you reserve airport parking, our team will know to expect you and make sure there is a spot available. Long-term parking is easily accommodated when you reserve on our website or mobile app.

Our customers expect the most convenient, most seamless, frictionless travel experience. That is why we provide easy access via our mobile app. Simply scan your code at the kiosk and park. Then, our friendly airport shuttle drivers will help you with your bag and get you on your way in minutes. In a hurry? Select our valet parking option and leave your keys with us. Some locations will charge your electric vehicle or wash your car while youre away. Explore premium parking and our car care options when you reserve your spot.

We provide covered airport parking if you want to protect your vehicle from the elements. Some facilities have extra-wide parking available for customers that need more space.

When you return, the mobile app tells you exactly where the shuttles are, reducing your waiting time. Our friendly drivers will drop you off right at your car. You wont get that at the airport economy lot!

It is our goal to provide our guests with hospitality, predictability, and convenience. We look forward to seeing you!
