About Us

Naba Inn is one of the best three- star standard luxury hotel located in the port city of Chattogram and strategically place at the heart of the city adjacent with GEC Circle. The hotel is only 10/15 minutes away from Chattogram Railway Station and 2 minutes away from main bus station(GEC). And 30 minutes away from Shah Amanat International Airport. Our hotel luxurious rooms which are outfitted with modern amenities. Cable TV, high speed WIFI,IDD and NWD telephones ,Free Newspaper, Free water, 24 – hours room service etc,24 hours electricity back up, individual safety box in room etc. Moreover we ensure international hospitality services that makes you sojourn at home while away from home. we have noiseless sound proof rooms . Hotel puts the following facilities and services at you disposal-a wide roof top restaurant, active GYM, mosque. For conferences- Corporate Events and Social Get- together, the spacious Mini Conference Room for small group, VIP meeting room for selective persons. We have more than mouth watering 4 types of Cuisine for Lunch and Dinner. So It’s just a home away home while we treating you like a family member.
