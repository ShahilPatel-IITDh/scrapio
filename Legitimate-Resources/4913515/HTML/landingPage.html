Crafted with Care

Our Story

We’re a close-knit team of creatives based in Andover, Massachusetts who use diamonds and gold to share stories.

Quality is our top priority and we present delicate, intentionally-designed jewelry, from our hands to yours.

Who is Melanie Casey?

Melanie Casey is our founder and jewelry designer. Being the skilled artisan that she is, Melanie carefully handcrafted her dream job. And things moved quickly.

In just over a decade, her passion for jewelry-making went from being a hobby to becoming a thriving operation that allows our team to make high-quality jewelry for thousands of people all over the world. Melanie Casey Fine Jewelry won the “Creative Concepts in Retailing Award” from the Retailers Association of Massachusetts in 2019. And in 2020 and 2021, Inc. Magazine recognized us as being one of the fastest-growing private companies in America.

Even though she now leads our company of expert goldsmiths, customer service reps, and dozens of other team members, Melanie is still very much involved in the jewelry-making journey of her dreams. She’s also a wife, a mom, and an Audible junkie, and she holds a degree in Finance and Marketing from Northeastern University.

Learn more

We present pieces of jewelry that are crafted with expertise and care, and our standards for quality and customer care are unmatched.

What You Get: Quality Craftsmanship

Sure, our expert jewelers handcraft diamond engagement rings, necklaces, bracelets, unique cluster rings, and men’s wedding bands. But they’re also storytellers who use diamonds and gold to tell your story.

Hand-set stones, polishing pieces the old way, and a three-step quality check process: we don’t do shortcuts.

We are perfectionists when it comes to the quality of your jewelry. This includes our commitment to care for the environment, to maintain high ethical standards, to practice sustainable sourcing methods, and to use recycled gold and other ethical materials.

With unmatched customer service, we’re committed to you for the lifetime of your jewelry, which we hope will become a family heirloom.
